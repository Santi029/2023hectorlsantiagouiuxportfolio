Development

After having gone through all the processes of research, analysis, ideation, wireframe, prototyping and everything is approved, the next step is the development process. At this point it is very important to have already incorporated the development team in all the meetings to make it part of the process. For them this is very important because they would be clear about how this application or solution is suppose to be built. Learn More>.

Test and Re-test

Transparent process. Complete overview of my design process through regular meetings. After the initial design versions I’m happy to do user testing with your customers to ensure the best possible solutions for their needs.

A/B Testing

In my experience A / B testing means that during the UI / UX process you will find or confront two or more solutions for the same component or segment and both could be really enjoyable for clients, co-workers, managers or stakeholders. If we have this situation and those two options survive the whole process, maybe, if the company has the time and resources to try all the different solutions, it will be great to present those options to the customer and they can decide the best option for them.

Hi, I'm Hector L. Santiago

And this is what I'm doing now

My name is Hector L. Santiago. I work as a UI/UX Designer for Copart International located in Dallas, Texas.

Currently, I’m designing the next generation of the Copart Website for US and two other subsidaries called CashforCars.com and CrashedToys.com with two of the best team in the company. Previously I was working as a Lead Manager for Amazon, Web advisor for Hostgator and many years as a web designer and developer in San Juan, Puerto Rico.

I am a dedicated and passionate UI / UX designer from Frisco, Texas. Since I started my learning journey as a web designer and web developer in 2008, I have enjoyed how design is created and transformed into a great website or app. I've always found it interesting how to create a layout from a user-centric approach and then bring it into code achieving the preset layout.

I have years of experience working with the Adobe Master Collection since version 3, along with programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The programs I use the most are Visual Studio Code, Git, GitHub, Illustrator, XD and recently Figma.

I proudly consider myself a lifelong learner in this industry because technology never stops.
